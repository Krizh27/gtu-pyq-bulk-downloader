About GTU Speedrun

GTU Speedrun is a fast, efficient archive for Gujarat Technological University (GTU) students to download Previous Year Question Papers (PYQ). It is designed for engineering, diploma, pharmacy, and management students who need quick access to exam resources, past papers, and question banks without navigating complex portals. Features include bulk downloading of summer and winter session papers by subject code.

Update: GTU introduced new subject codes from the 2025–26 curriculum. Our website supports both old and new subject codes automatically!

Tip: Even if a subject code is new or unmapped, entering it with the correct year range will still fetch your papers perfectly!
